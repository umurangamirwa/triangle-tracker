Definition of a triangle

A triangle is a plane figure with three straight sides and three angles.

Types of a triangle

  • Equilateral
  • Iscoceless
  • Scalene

Type definitions

Equilateral

An equilateral,is a triangle that has all sides equal.

Iscoceless

An isolateral,is a triangle in which two sides have the same length.

Scalene

A scalene,is a triangle triangle that has three unequal sides.
